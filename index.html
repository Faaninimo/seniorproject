More about our programs

We have two mentorships that are meant for students to get help either academically with tips and tricks from students that are older like juniors and seniors kind of like a support system for the student in their academic journey. Or professionally where students are paired up with someone who is in the profession that they are wanting to go into after they graduate. But this person can also help the student look for internships and help guide them in the right direction when it comes to working in the feild. Both of our programs are active at different times of the year.
